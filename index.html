Introduction

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at the CAS Key Laboratory of AI Security, advised by Prof. Xueqi Cheng and Assoc. Prof. Bingbing Xu. I obtained my B.S. degree in Information Security from Xidian University in 2020.

My research interests span the areas of machine learning, especially in machine learning generalization, physics-inspired machine learning and deep generative models. Looking ahead, I am also considering expanding my research to include the exploration of LLM agents.
