Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student at CAS Key Laboratory of AI Security, advised by Prof. Xueqi Cheng and Assoc. Prof. Bingbing Xu. I received my B.S. degree of Iformation Security from Xidian University in 2020.

My research interests span the areas of machine learning, specially in physics-inspired machine learning, machine learning generalization and graph representation learning.
